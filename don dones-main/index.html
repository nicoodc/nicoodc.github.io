Special Dish

Special Bibingka

Special bibingka is a traditional Filipino rice cake made with galapong (rice flour dough), coconut milk, eggs, sugar, and grated cheese. It is usually cooked in banana leaves and served warm with butter or margarine on top. The texture is moist and dense, with a slightly sweet and savory taste that is perfect for breakfast or a mid-day snack. It is a must-try Filipino delicacy that will surely satisfy your taste buds!
